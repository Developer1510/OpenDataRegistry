CORINE Land Cover

Short description

In 1985 the 'Coordination of Information on the Environment' (CORINE) programme was initiated by the European Commission. It aimed at collecting environmental information on high priority topics for the European Union (air, water, soil, land cover, coastal erosion, biotopes, etc.). Since 1994, the established databases and programmes are managed by the European Environment Agency (EEA).The CORINE Land Cover (CLC) inventory is a vector-based dataset that consists of 44 land cover and land use classes (Table 2) derived from a series of satellite missions since it was first established (Table 1).Table 1: The evolution of CORINE Land Cover (source).
